### About Me

Hey there! I'm an embedded systems enthusiast based in Tianjin, passionate about building things that bridge hardware and software.

- **Embedded** — C/C++ on bare-metal and RTOS, PCB design, digital power control
- **Control Systems** — PID tuning, motor modeling, sensor fusion
- **Software** — Full-stack tinkering, simulation, and tooling

> *"A person who likes something interesting."*
